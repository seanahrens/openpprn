OpenPPRN

The Open-Source, Patient-Centric, Mobile-First Patient-Powered Research Network

View project onGitHub

In the push of a button, deploy a PPRN (Patient-Powered Research Network) that is complete with a:

Patient Registry with Universal Portable Informed Consent
Health Data Visualizer & Tracking App
Research Survey Platform
Patient Social Network

Open

Open Source. Open Data. No vendor lock-in. The data is owned by the patients, stewarded by the patient advocacy group. Move your network at any time.

Patient-Centric

Designed for the best interest of Patients with a focus on engaging user-experience to have patients voluntarily sign up on their own. Patients fill out once a Universal Portable Informed Consent. Patient Health Data is aggregated from hundreds of apps.

Mobile-First

The whole user experience is designed mobile-first. Patient registration, research surveys, and health tracking are designed to be done from the patient's mobile phone, first.

The Features

Patient Registry with Universal Portable Informed Consent.

Patients register for your PPRN to learn more about their condition, learn and contribute to current research, and (optionally) learn and/or socialize with other patients. Up front, patients sign a Universal Portable Informed Consent, authorizing their data contribution to research projects prospectively.

Health Data Visualization & Tracking Tools

Patients see a Visual Timeline of their Health Record and can add Real-Time updates as symptoms and treatments change.

Run Research Surveys

Researchers can distribute surveys out to the patients and segment who receives them based on the patients' health record data. Return findings directly back to patients.

Patient Social Network

Patients can see other patients on a map, and see a listing of support groups / patient meetups near them. And ask and answer questions of one another.
