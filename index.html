OpenPPRN

The Free, Open, Patient-First Website every Patient Advocacy Organization should use

In the push of a button, deploy your free, open PPRN that is complete with a:

Patient Registry 
Health Data Visualizer & Tracking App
Research Survey Platform
Patient Social Network

Collect, Engage, and Empower Patients in Research

Designed for the best interest of Patients and Patient Advocacy Groups. With a focus on engaging user-experience to have patients voluntarily sign up on their own.

Mobile-First

The whole user experience is designed mobile-first. Patient registration, research surveys, and health tracking are designed to be done from the patient's mobile phone, first.

Patients Own the Data

Patients fill out once a Universal Portable Informed Consent. Patient Health Data is aggregated from hundreds of apps. Patients own their data and authorize approved research usage.

Open-Source

No vendor lock-in. The data is owned by the patients, stewarded by the patient advocacy group. Move your network at any time.

The Components

Privacy-First Patient Registry

Patients register for your PPRN to learn more about their condition, learn and contribute to current research, and (optionally) learn and/or socialize with other patients.

Health Data Visualization & Tracking Tools

Patients see a Visual Timeline of their Health Record and can add Real-Time updates as symptoms and treatments change.

Run Research Surveys

Researchers can distribute surveys out to the patients and segment who receives them based on the patients' health record data. Return findings directly back to patients.

Patient Social Network

Patients can see other patients on a map, and see a listing of support groups / patient meetups near them. And ask and answer questions of one another.
