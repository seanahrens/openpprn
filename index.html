OpenPPRN

The Open-Source, Patient-Centric, Mobile-First Patient-Powered Research Network

View project onGitHub

In the push of a button, deploy a PPRN (Patient-Powered Research Network) that is complete with a:

Patient Registry
Health Data Aggregation, Visualization, & Tracking
Research Survey Platform
Patient Social Network

Philosophy

Open

Open Source. Open Data. No vendor lock-in. The data is owned by the patients, stewarded by the patient advocacy group. Move your network at any time.

Patient-Centric

Designed for the best interest of Patients with a focus on engaging user-experience to have patients voluntarily sign up on their own. Patients fill out once a Universal Portable Informed Consent. Patient Health Data is aggregated from hundreds of apps.

Mobile-First

The whole user experience is designed mobile-first. Patient registration, research surveys, and health tracking are designed to be done from the patient's mobile phone, first.

Consent Once. Research Forever.

Up front, patients sign a Universal Portable Informed Consent, authorizing their data contribution to research projects prospectively.

Features

Patient Registry

Patients register for your PPRN to learn more about their condition, learn and contribute to current research, and (optionally) learn and/or socialize with other patients.

Health Data Aggregation, Visualization, & Tracking

In the click of a few buttons, patients can authorize incoming data streams from hundreds of mobile health apps and participating EHR systems. Patients see a visual timeline of their health record and from their mobile phone can add real-time updates as symptoms and treatments change. Researchers can crunch this data to do things like perform predictive models of disease.

Run Research Surveys

Researchers can distribute surveys out to the patients and segment who receives them based on the patients' health record data. Return findings directly back to patients.

Patient Social Network

Patients can see other patients on a map, and see a listing of support groups / patient meetups near them. And ask and answer questions of one another.

Other Benefits

Your website

Your domain. Your content and branding. Your deploy will be hosted on your own domain (ie. ALDConnect.org) and your own branding, colors, and content will make your OpenPPRN yours.

Co-Growing Database of Patients

You'll benefit from all other participating OpenPPRNs. Patients that land on the master OpenPPRN domain will choose their condition and get directed to your PPRN. Patients who register for other OpenPPRNs who have your condition will get registered with you.

Search-Engine Optimized

Popular searches by patients with your condition will reveal your OpenPPRN in Google and other search engines.

Patient Verification Workflow

A workflow to verify the diagnosis of patients who sign up for your registry is built-in.
